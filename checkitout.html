What is MyClub?

To put it simply, MyClub allows you to create a working dashboard for clubs and organizations. We provide several tools and features that allows you connect with other organizations, plan out your next big event, and handle all the messy tedious tasks like finances and member accountability lists. Don't worry you won't have do manage it all alone either, you can send private invitations to staff members of your organization and work together!

Here is some cool features that MyClub has to offer!

  • Financial management
  • Member lists
  • Staff member discussion forum
  • Private invitation system
  • Form alliances, and coalitions with other organizations
